🇬🇧 ENGLISH

01. Mode Selection (CALC / MUSIC)

Use the top-left light-yellow button to toggle between Calculation and Music modes. The bottom-left of the LCD will indicate your current mode. Note that sounds are synthesized in both modes.

02. Recording Music (REC MEM)

In Music mode, press [REC MEM] to start recording. The button will have a pulsing red border. Play your melody using the keypad (numbers and operators act as different musical notes). Press [REC MEM] again to stop and save your sequence.

03. Playback & Speed (PLAY MEM)

Press [PLAY MEM] to play back your recorded sequence. The button text will turn green during playback. You can press [PLAY MEM] again at any time to stop. Use the [Speed] button to cycle through playback speeds (0.5x to 4x).

04. THREE-SLOT MEMORY SYSTEM (NEW!)

Press the [MEM SLOT] button to cycle between three independent memory slots (MEM SLOT 1, SLOT 2, SLOT 3). The LCD will display the current slot for 1 second. Each slot can store a different melody sequence!

05. LOOP MODE (NEW!)

Press [Loop Off] to activate loop mode. A modal will appear asking you to select how many times to loop (2-5). The button will show a yellow border and change to "Loop On" when active. Your sequence will repeat the selected number of times during playback.

06. IO Sequence Editor (Import / Export)

Press the [IO] button to open the Memory Data Transfer menu. Here you can view your recorded sequence as text (e.g., 123+45.).

• Export: Click [COPY] to copy the sequence to your clipboard and share it.
• Import/Edit: You can type directly into the box or click [PASTE] to paste a sequence from elsewhere. Click [IMPORT] (the green button) to load this sequence into the synthesizer's memory.

07. Memory, Deletion & Return

• [Del Rec] / [AC]: Clears the entire recorded memory (Music mode) or clears the calculator screen (Calc mode).
• [RETURN]: Acts as a backspace, deleting the last entered note or digit.

08. Special Key

Pressing the [SPECIAL KEY] at the bottom will automatically play a snippet of Kraftwerk's Pocket Calculator / Taschenrechner. The play button turns green during playback. You can interrupt and stop playback at any time by pressing [PLAY MEM].

HK 繁體中文

01. 模式切換 (計算機 / 音樂)

使用左上角的淺黃色按鈕在計算機 (CALC)與音樂 (MUSIC)模式之間切換。螢幕左下方會顯示當前模式。請注意，兩種模式下按鍵皆會發出合成音效。

02. 錄製音樂 (REC MEM)

在音樂模式下，按下 [REC MEM] 開始錄音，按鈕會出現紅色脈動邊框。使用鍵盤彈奏旋律（數字與加減乘除代表不同音符）。再次按下 [REC MEM] 即可停止並儲存您的序列。

03. 播放與速度調整 (PLAY MEM)

按下 [PLAY MEM] 播放您錄製的旋律，播放時按鈕字體會變為綠色。您可以隨時再次按下該鍵來停止播放。使用 [Speed] 按鈕可切換播放速度（0.5倍至4倍）。

04. 三插槽記憶體系統 (新功能!)

按下 [MEM SLOT] 按鈕可在三個獨立的記憶插槽間切換 (MEM SLOT 1, SLOT 2, SLOT 3)。LCD螢幕將顯示當前插槽1秒鐘。每個插槽可以儲存不同的旋律序列！

05. 循環模式 (新功能!)

按下 [Loop Off] 啟動循環模式。將出現對話框要求您選擇循環次數 (2-5次)。啟動後按鈕會顯示黃色邊框並變更為「Loop On」。您的序列將在播放時重複選定的次數。

06. IO 序列編輯器 (匯入 / 匯出)

按下 [IO] 按鈕開啟記憶體數據傳輸選單。您可以在此以純文字查看錄製的音符序列（例如：123+45.）。

• 匯出 (Export)：點擊 [COPY] 將序列複製到剪貼簿以便分享。
• 匯入與編輯 (Import/Edit)：您可以直接在文字框內修改，或點擊 [PASTE] 貼上外部序列。完成後點擊綠色的 [IMPORT] 按鈕，即可將該旋律載入合成器的記憶體中。

07. 刪除與返回鍵

• [Del Rec] / [AC]：在音樂模式下清除所有錄音記憶；在計算機模式下為歸零。
• [RETURN]：作為退格鍵使用，刪除上一個輸入的音符或數字。

08. 特殊彩蛋鍵 (SPECIAL KEY)

按下底部的 [SPECIAL KEY]，機器將自動演奏一段 Kraftwerk（發電廠樂團）的名曲 Pocket Calculator / Taschenrechner。播放按鈕會在播放時變為綠色。若要中斷播放，請隨時按下 [PLAY MEM]。
