# technopopcalculator
A neon-infused, browser-based tribute to the minimalist genius of Kraftwerk and the golden era of pocket synthesizers. The Technopop RS-2026A features a dual-mode interface, a high-capacity 1,500-note sequence memory, and a programmable speed engine (up to 4x). Now with instant-share URLs to export your compositions. Step into the 'Pocket Calculator' aesthetic with shocking green feedback and retro digital synthesis. 🎹⚡️🤖
